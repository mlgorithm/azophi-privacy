# Azophi — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 20 June 2026_

**Azophi** is a just-for-fun radar that shows the aircraft and satellites passing
overhead. This policy explains how Azophi handles your information. In short:
**Azophi does not collect, store, or share your personal data.**

## No data collection

Azophi has **no account system, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no
servers operated by the developer**. Everything runs on your device. We do not
collect, store, sell, or share any personal information about you.

## Location

If you allow it, Azophi uses your device's location to centre the radar on you and
to look up the aircraft currently near you. Your location:

- is used **on your device** to draw the radar, and
- is included **only** in real-time requests to the public flight-data service
  **airplanes.live** (e.g. "which aircraft are within this radius of me?"), so it
  can return the planes around you.

Your location is **not** stored by the app, **not** linked to your identity, and
**not** used to track you. If you deny location access, Azophi falls back to a
default city.

## Services Azophi contacts

To show live information, Azophi requests data **directly** from the third-party
services below. Your device contacts them directly; as with any internet request,
they receive your IP address (and, for aircraft, your approximate location) only to
fulfil that request. Their handling of that information is governed by their own
policies:

- **airplanes.live** — live aircraft positions
- **CelesTrak** — satellite orbital data
- **CARTO / OpenStreetMap** — map tiles
- **NASA GIBS** and **EOX** — satellite imagery
- **RainViewer** — weather-radar imagery

Azophi sends these services nothing beyond what is technically required to make each
request, and receives nothing about you in return.

## Children

Azophi is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. It does not collect data from
anyone, including children.
